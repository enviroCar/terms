Data Privacy Statement

enviroCar is an open Citizen Science platform that enables citizens, scientists and traffic engineers to collect data from moving vehicles and use this data to answer questions about the efficiency and sustainability of traffic.

The use of the enviroCar.org Website and the enviroCar App requires the creation, processing and storing of personal data.

Personal data is information that directly or indirectly relates to the identity of a person (eg. name, email address, IP address, etc.).

We are aware of our responsibility to protect personal data and keep them confidential according to the current data protection regulations. In the following paragraphs, we would like to provide information about which data we collect and for what purpose we use it

Your right to information, correction, blocking, deletion

You have the right to request information about what kind of personal data have been stored about you and for what purpose it is used. You also have a right to correction or deletion of your personal data, or to restrict its processing as well as a right of objection against its processing. The right of objection can also apply to a certain processing or usage of your personal data.

You can object to the usage of your personal data at any time by letter or by e-mail to privacy@52north.org.

You also have the right to receive the pertinent personal data which you have provided to us in a structured, well established and machine-readable format. At your request, we will forward your request to other responsible instances, designated by you, as far as this is technically feasible.

In the event that you do not feel sufficiently supported by our company in your rights, you have the right to file a complaint with the State Representative for Data Protection and Freedom of Information North Rhine-Westphalia (www.ldi.nrw.de).

b) Publication of the data using the smartphone app and analysis of the data on the enviroCar server

By uploading data from the enviroCar app to the enviroCar server (option: 'Upload data as open data'), you agree to have your driving data published in an anonymous form as open data and that we and third parties may use it in accordance with the Open Database License (ODbL). See for more information: https://www.opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/summary/index.html

You help scientists and traffic planners gain important data for their work when you publish your data. At the same time, uploading it enables you to use the enviroCar platform's advanced capabilities to analyze your own data.

Your enviroCar account contains a reference to all the tracks you have published as Open Data. As a result, you have the opportunity to manage your own data and analyze it using the platform's resources. You can delete some or all of your driving data from the enviroCar platform at any time. You also have the option to delete your account. All driving data, which have already been downloaded by third parties as Open Data, can still be used by them in accordance with the ODbL.


c) Use of the published data by third parties

The data that you have uploaded to the enviroCar server (option: 'Upload driving data as open data') can be downloaded from there via various interfaces may be used by third parties in accordance with the Open Database License (ODbL). We permit the copying of the data, its distribution and the derivation of new data from the database.

When disclosing the data to third parties, you must specify where the data came from ('Source: enviroCar contributors'). Each copied, modified or supplemented version of the database must be made accessible itself in accordance with the ODbL. The ODbL’s binding text can be found here: https://www.opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1.0/

There is no reference to your account in the driving data that you publish as open data, thus other users have no information about this relationship. Please note, it may be possible to draw conclusions about the author without this information. The data itself may contain such individual characteristics that a relationship to specific persons is obvious. For example, this is the case when a rare type of vehicle moves between a person’s living and working locations and this person is known to have this type of vehicle.To complicate such conclusions, there is an option to adjust the data recording so that it starts and ends with a minimum distance or duration from the start or destination of your journey.


Cookies

We use cookies to optimize our websites and to enhance your user experience.

Cookies are small text files that your browser stores when you visit web pages and applications. Your browser downloads the cookies the first time you visit a particular website. When you revisit this website with the same device, the data stored in the cookie is sent back to the website that created it. The website recognizes the web browser that invoked it and optimizes the content displayed for you accordingly. You can specify how websites use cookies through the privacy settings in your browser (for more information on configuring cookie settings, refer to the help function of your browser). If you disable cookies altogether, the enviroCar websites and applications may no longer function correctly under certain circumstances. More information about cookies can be found at: https://www.allaboutcookies.org/
